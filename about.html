Our Story

Tracing the thread of tradition, design innovation, and fashion expertise.

Established 1979

A Heritage of Elegant Fitting

Maya Collection brings modern and traditional fashion together with carefully selected clothing designed for comfort, confidence, and affordability. For over four decades, we have been tailoring dreams and styling realities for our valued clientele across Bengaluru.

What started as a boutique design workshop has evolved into a premier destination for traditional ethnic wear, designer bridal options, and modern casual attire. We believe elegance should be accessible to everyone without compromising on material quality.

Our Vision

To be the premier boutique in Bengaluru, known for blending classic handcrafted design with contemporary elegance across all sizes.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality fashion that builds customer confidence and trust, offering seamless physical and online retail experiences.

Our Promise

Every textile goes through structured quality control checks, backed by our accommodating 7-day exchange program.

Our Milestone Timeline

1979

The Creative Seed

Opened a small bespoke tailoring boutique in Bengaluru focusing on high-quality bridal fabrics.

1995

Store Expansion

Moved to our larger showroom location to accommodate physical off-the-rack designer dresses and ethnic wear collections.

2012

Size Inclusivity Initiative

Introduced our highly requested extended-fit and dedicated Plus Size Festive wear collections.

2026

Modern Digital Identity

Launched an integrated catalog experience combining local personalized physical fittings with digital product showcases.
